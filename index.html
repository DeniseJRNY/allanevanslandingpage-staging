Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during the complimentary discovery call?

The discovery call is a confidential 15-minute conversation to understand your situation, answer initial questions, and determine whether our Premium Family Law service is the right fit for your matter. There is absolutely no obligation.

Do I have to go to court?

Not necessarily. We prioritise negotiation and mediation to resolve matters efficiently and with less stress. However, if court proceedings become necessary, we provide robust representation with access to leading family law barristers.

How quickly can I get started?

We're available to get started this week. After your discovery call and engagement, a strategic conference with a Senior Lawyer and Barrister occurs within 7–14 days. You'll receive clear written advice and a roadmap shortly after.

What types of matters does Premium Family Law handle?

We handle significant family law matters including property and financial settlements, children's and parenting arrangements, separation and divorce, spousal maintenance, pre-nuptial agreements, and court proceedings. We represent married, de facto, and same-sex couples.

How are your fees structured?

We provide complete transparency around costs from the outset. You'll receive a detailed costs agreement before any work begins. There are no hidden fees or surprise invoices. This is discussed in detail during your discovery call.

Will I deal directly with a senior lawyer?

Yes. Premium Family Law is a senior-led service. Your matter receives close senior oversight from Director Mary Pollatos, with direct access to experienced barristers when needed. You won't be handed off to junior staff.
